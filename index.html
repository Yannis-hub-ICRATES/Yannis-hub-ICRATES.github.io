Welcome to the Cyprus subset!


In the following screens, different aspects of the ICRATES data from Cyprus are visualized.

In the lefthand column, you can find an overview of all records associated with each of the 11 sites, as well as visualizations regarding the origins of the data, such as the sites, the deposits, and the publications.

In the righthand column, the focus lies on the tablewares present in the dataset, and especially their distribution over the sites and over time.
