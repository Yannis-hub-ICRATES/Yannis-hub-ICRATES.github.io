In the right-hand column, the focus lies on the tablewares present in the dataset, and especially their distribution over the sites and over time. Terminology:

Catalogue
In a catalogue, the individual ceramics and ceramic types are described.
Quantification
In a quantification, the ceramics and ceramic types are summarized.
Location
A specific place or sector within a site.
Deposit
An archaeological unit, like a fill, a debris layer, a dump, ...

Fabric
The tableware a sherd belongs to, usually characterized by a specific (chemical) composition.
Standard Form
The standard typological form of the sherd in question, as defined in the typochronology used in the dataset.

(*) The 'main' fabric groups are those for which a typochronology was available and incorporated into the database. For more details, see the ICRATES database web pages.
